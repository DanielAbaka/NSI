  • About Me

If there is anyone in town who knows exactly how to fire your Mind up and open it to work like a parachute; Godwin is the Man.

The Big Godwin; (as he is often called) a Multi-Gifted President of the Soft Group Ltd has an efficacious countenance that makes everything around him come to life. A Motivational Speaker, Leader, Mentor, I.T Consultant and a passionate Saxophone Player is a young gentleman with an Entrepreneurial MindSet. By 2008, Godwin had Lectured in one of the most Respected Polytechnics in Ghana where he finally resigned as a Head of Section.

His passion for Entrepreneurship has drilled depth in his understanding of business processes and growing SMES in the Sub Saharan Africa, transforming him from a Core I.T Personnel to a Highly sort-after Business Consultant. He has consulted for many Banks and other MultiNationals. He is currently the C.E.O of WEBSoft Solutions (an I.T Firm that builds products and provides Services to Banks and other corporate Institutions, and the Managing Director of the HairSoft Solutions; a hair brand for women, 100% human virgin hair, and also runs a hair grooming salon. He is also the CEO of AgroSoft Solutions, a coy with hectares of farms with the focus on growing crops for local sale and exports.

Godwin was adjudged a unique achiever by Joy FM in 2014 and was also featured on Joy News Personality Profile, TV3 Morning Show and several radio interviews. He runs a "Butterfly your World" online project (using Facebook as a Social Media Tool: https://www.facebook.com/BigGodwin) whose focus is solely to inspire the world through Faith in the Word of God. He continues to share daily wisdom nuggets and confronting outdated thought-patterns with new information that is changing how people perceive the world and their own lives.

He holds a BSc in computer science and furthered to acquire a Post Graduate Diploma in MIS from GIMPA.
